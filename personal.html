This Issue's Affect on me

In my opinion, this issue of traditional food dishes becoming less popular is an important issue on the global scale, however I do not think it is really of that much significance to me personally. I think this because having been born and growing up in an international city such as Hong Kong in the 21st century, there hasn't been a time where I haven't been in touch or in contact with more than one culture. Not only does living in Hong Kong mean such a diverse cultural exposure, but in this decade, the massive technological advancements have meant that we can always be in touch with a another place.

What I mean by this is, because I have never experienced or been in a monoculutral exnvironment, I feel that this issue does not really have that great of an impact or effect on me. This bit of the issue is very deeply rooted in the idea of preserving traditions, and I haven't been brought up in a way that traditions are that important to me.
