What is the impact of this issue on Hong Kong and how are the people affected by it?

Cultural destruction or cultrral enhancement?

I wouldn’t say this issue has big impact on HK, because HK identity based on being global city. On the contrary, I think having enormous number of different global foods in HK enhances rich culture and its diversity. However, that is not to say HK does not have own traditional cuisine. I would say food people eat in HK affected/influenced in equal amounts by local dishes as well as international food.
