The Future

Scenarios, Solutions and implications

Future Scenarios

What might happen in the future? Could the issue get worse?

With this issue, there are a few things I think will definitely happen:

  • Very big hybridisation and modification of countries’ food culture
  • Countries' food culture in ≈20 years time will be very different to what they are like now
  • A few countries’ food culture may become completely forgotten because of American fast food chains

But are these future scenarios bad or good? Some will say that this is a bad thing because of not sticking to traditions, while others would argue that change is good.

Solutions & Implications

How could the problems be solved? What are the practial problems with your suggestions?

So the issue here that some deem problematic is that traditional food culture will be changed/affected in a bad way, because of factors such as globalisation, and American fast food.

One possible solution to this problem is cut down on the number of fast food chains while at the same time promoting a particular traditional dish of that country. By promote, I mean do whatever that can be done to raise popularity or awareness of the dish to get people (back) into that dish. For example, discounts on that dish in any restaurants where it is sold, cooking competitions exclusively on cooking traditional dishes, etc. However, the problem with this suggestion is of the scale: you cannot shut down that large number of fast food restaurants, nor can you carry out the dish promotion in every city of the country.
